Located off North Palm Canyon Drive, Mediterraneo Resort is minutes from the entertainment of downtown Palm Springs, with golf courses, casinos, shopping, hiking, and the Palm Springs Aerial Tramway, which was recently voted in the top 5 best trams in the world, located less than a 5 minute drive away. Offering guests majestic mountain views while sunbathing in either of the two pools, the resorts onsite restaurant offers poolside service and a full bar menu.

Based upon your needs and desires you can choose from our Studio, Junior, One-bedroom or Two-Bedroom Suites. All of our suites are non-smoking and every room at Mediterraneo Resort offers full kitchens.  Unlike many of the other resorts in Palm Springs, our guests recieve free parking and Wifi!  

Guests can choose between a Standard room or a Deluxe for each room type.  Deluxe rooms are upgraded with modern furnishings, large screen TV's and designer decor. 

Guests who book directly through our website are guaranteed the best pricing available!
